Security Services

Professional guarding
and site protection.

SIA-licensed personnel deployed to protect your people, assets and premises. Cover is designed around your site — not a standard template.

Security

Manned Guarding

Static and mobile guard posts for live sites. Visible deterrence, access management and incident reporting as standard. Suitable for commercial, industrial and high-footfall environments.

Security

Mobile Patrols

Regular patrol rounds for sites that need periodic checks rather than a permanent presence. Reduces risk cost-effectively while maintaining visible security cover.

Security

Keyholding & Alarm Response

Out-of-hours response to alarm activations, with trained personnel attending site. Takes the burden off staff and ensures a professional response every time.

Security

Gatehouse & Access Control

Managed entry and exit for industrial, commercial and restricted access sites. Contractor control, visitor logging and access enforcement — professionally managed.

Security

CCTV Monitoring

Remote and on-site camera monitoring with incident logging and escalation. Provides continuous coverage where a constant physical presence is not practical.

Security

Event Security

Professional security staffing for events, exhibitions and temporary gatherings. Flexible deployment at short notice with full briefing and clear reporting lines.

Facilities Services

Site support that keeps
operations running.

Facilities cover that reduces disruption, maintains standards and removes the need for multiple separate contractors.

Facilities

Cleaning Support

Regular and one-off cleaning for commercial and industrial premises. Scheduled programmes or reactive cover — to the standard your site requires.

Facilities

Site Support Services

General site support including waste, presentation and day-to-day operational tasks. Reduces the burden on your own staff while keeping the site running properly.

Facilities

Maintenance Coordination

Liaising with contractors, managing access and ensuring maintenance works proceed without disruption to your operations. One point of contact for multiple trades.

Facilities

Front-of-House Support

Reception, visitor management and first-point-of-contact staffing. Professional presentation for client-facing environments without the overhead of permanent staff.

Facilities

Void & Vacant Property Checks

Regular inspections, reporting and security for unoccupied properties. Prevents deterioration, detects problems early and provides documentation for insurance purposes.
