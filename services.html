What We Provide

Security and facilities services
for operational sites.

TTS FM provides SIA-licensed security and integrated facilities management for commercial, industrial and operational environments. All services delivered by professional, vetted personnel under direct management.

Security Services

SIA-licensed guarding
and site protection.

All front-line security services are delivered by SIA-licensed, vetted personnel. Deployed with site-specific briefings and clear reporting structures.

Manned Guarding

Static security officers deployed at fixed posts on commercial, industrial and public-facing sites. Access control, incident deterrence and first-response responsibilities.

Mobile Patrol

Vehicle-based patrol rounds across single or multiple sites. Regular security checks, incident reporting and patrol logs maintained throughout each shift.

Keyholding & Alarm Response

Out-of-hours keyholding and alarm response for client sites. Reliable, rapid attendance to alarm activations with documented response records.

Access Control & Gatehouse

Managed entry points for industrial, commercial and construction sites. Contractor sign-in, visitor management, access logs and site security procedures.

Event Security

SIA-licensed security staffing for events, festivals and temporary sites. Short-notice deployment available. Crowd management, front-of-house and perimeter roles.

Vacant Property Security

Regular inspections and security patrols for unoccupied properties. Documented reports after each visit covering access points, utilities and building fabric.

Facilities Services

Site support that keeps
operations running.

Facilities management services delivered to a consistent professional standard. Managed alongside security services or as a standalone contract.

Commercial Cleaning

Regular and deep cleaning for offices, industrial facilities, public-facing premises and managed sites. Scheduled and responsive cleaning available.

Front-of-House Support

Reception, concierge and visitor management support for commercial premises. Professional presentation and direct communication as standard.

Void Property Management

Inspection, maintenance coordination and ongoing management for vacant or void properties. Full documentation maintained for insurance and compliance purposes.

Maintenance Coordination

Coordination and oversight of reactive and planned maintenance works. Single point of contact for FM requirements across one or multiple sites.

Site Support Operatives

General site support staff for a range of facilities tasks. Reliable, managed operatives deployed with clear instructions and consistent standards.

Integrated FM Package

Security and facilities management combined under one contract. Simplified operations, single point of contact and clear accountability across all site services.

Sectors We Support

Sites across the public
and private sector.

TTS FM has experience across a range of operational environments. Our personnel are briefed specifically for each site type.

Industrial & Manufacturing

Security and site support for live manufacturing and industrial environments. Access control, contractor management and incident reporting for sites where continuity of operations is critical.

Commercial Property & Business Parks

Front-of-house, guarding, cleaning and facilities management for office buildings, business parks and managed commercial premises.

NHS & Healthcare Estates

Manned guarding and fire watch patrols across NHS estate sites. Reliable, professional personnel who support existing security teams and operate to healthcare standards.

Ports & Logistics

Security and access management for port operations and logistics facilities. Experience in high-volume, multi-contractor environments with strict access requirements.

Construction & Development Sites

Gatehouse operations, mobile patrols and perimeter security for construction sites. Protection of materials, plant and access points throughout the build programme.

Vacant & Void Property

Inspection, patrol and management services for unoccupied properties. Documented records maintained for insurance purposes with clear escalation procedures in place from day one.
