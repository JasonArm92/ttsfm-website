TTS FM (UK) is a local, regional and nationwide provider of integrated, risk-based security solutions and facilities management services. Our unique ‘One Stop Shop’ approach brings security and facilities management together under a single accountable partner — reducing your management overhead, driving operational efficiency, and giving every site one point of contact. We use our insight and knowledge to create the most appropriate service solution for each client, taking into account the risk profile and culture of the organisation.
