How Our Grounds Maintenance Process Works

Our grounds maintenance service is built around a structured process that ensures every site receives the right level of care at the right time — year-round, without interruption.

1. Site Survey
We begin with a detailed survey of your grounds, assessing the scope of work required, identifying any immediate maintenance needs, and evaluating seasonal considerations that will shape your service programme.
2. Annual Maintenance Plan
Based on the site survey, we produce a structured annual maintenance plan that outlines scheduled visits, seasonal tasks, and reactive service provisions — giving you full visibility and control over your grounds maintenance programme.
3. Seasonal Service Delivery
Our grounds teams deliver services in line with seasonal demands — from spring and summer grass cutting and planting to autumn leaf clearance and winter gritting and snow removal. Each visit is documented and reported.
4. Reporting & Review
We provide regular service reports and hold periodic review meetings to assess performance, adapt the service plan to changing needs, and ensure your grounds are consistently maintained to the agreed standard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ground maintenance services does TTS FM provide?
TTS FM provides a comprehensive range of grounds maintenance services including grass cutting and lawn care, weed control and treatment, hedge and tree trimming, hard landscaping, seasonal planting, winter gritting and snow clearance, leaf clearance, and site-specific grounds management programmes.
Do you offer winter gritting services?
Yes. TTS FM provides professional winter gritting and snow clearance services for commercial and industrial sites across the UK. Our winter management service includes reactive gritting, pre-planned gritting schedules, snow clearance, and full documentation of all treatments for compliance purposes.
How are your grounds maintenance schedules structured?
We work with each client to develop an annual grounds maintenance plan that reflects the specific needs of their site and the seasonal demands of their environment. Schedules are agreed in advance and can be adjusted throughout the year to accommodate changing requirements.
Can you manage multiple sites across the UK?
Yes. TTS FM has the regional coverage and operational infrastructure to deliver consistent grounds maintenance services across multiple sites anywhere in the UK, managed through a single point of contact and supported by detailed site-by-site reporting.
Are your grounds teams trained and qualified?
Absolutely. All TTS FM grounds operatives are trained in the safe operation of grounds maintenance equipment and comply with relevant health and safety legislation. Our teams hold appropriate certifications and are fully insured for all grounds maintenance activities.
