How Our Security Service Works

We follow a structured, consultative process to ensure every security contract is built on a clear understanding of your risk — and delivered with the supervision and reporting that keep standards high.

2. Tailored Security Plan
We design a bespoke solution and clear assignment instructions, blending manned guarding, mobile patrols, key holding and electronic monitoring to match your risk profile and budget.

We deploy SIA-licensed, BS 7858-screened officers who are fully briefed on your site. Dedicated, consistent teams build familiarity, ownership and a visible deterrent.

Our 24/7 control room and field supervisors maintain standards through patrol verification, spot checks and incident management — with transparent digital reporting and a single point of contact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TTS FM an SIA Approved Contractor?

Yes. TTS FM is an SIA Approved Contractor, the highest standard of accreditation in the UK private security industry. Every officer we deploy holds a valid SIA licence and is screened to BS 7858, and our management systems are independently assured to ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001.

What security services do you provide?

We provide a full range of security services including manned guarding, mobile patrols, key holding and alarm response, CCTV and remote monitoring, door supervision, and event and concierge security. Services can be delivered individually or as part of an integrated facilities management contract.

Do you offer 24/7 monitoring and response?

Yes. Our control room operates 24 hours a day, every day of the year, coordinating patrols, alarm response and incident management. This ensures a rapid, documented response to any security event, supported by real-time patrol verification.

Can security be combined with your other facilities services?

Absolutely. Our 'One Stop Shop' model lets you combine security with cleaning, maintenance, grounds and wider FM under a single contract and one point of contact — reducing cost, complexity and the overhead of managing multiple suppliers.

How quickly can you mobilise a new contract?

Mobilisation timescales depend on the size and complexity of the site, but our regional teams and established vetting processes allow us to deploy quickly. Following a site survey and risk assessment, we agree a clear mobilisation plan and assignment instructions before officers go live.
