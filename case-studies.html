Deployments

How TTS FM supports
operational sites.

The scenarios below represent typical TTS FM deployments. Client details have been anonymised.

Industrial

Access control and guarding for a live manufacturing site

Challenge

A North East manufacturer needed round-the-clock access management and visible security cover during a period of increased site activity. Contractor volumes were high and existing procedures were creating access log gaps.

Response

TTS FM deployed a static guard with gatehouse responsibilities, managing contractor access and maintaining a detailed site log. Personnel were briefed specifically on site procedures and escalation protocols.

Reliable cover maintained throughout. No access incidents reported.
Vacant Property

Patrol and inspection service for a vacant commercial building

Challenge

A property management company needed regular checks on an unoccupied building to monitor for damage, unauthorised access or deterioration. Insurance requirements demanded documented inspection records.

Response

TTS FM provided scheduled mobile patrol visits with written inspection reports after each visit, covering all access points, utilities and building fabric. Escalation procedures were in place from day one.

Early identification of a drainage issue prevented further damage. Full documentation maintained for insurance purposes.
Events & Temporary

Short-notice security cover for a North East event

Challenge

An event organiser needed trained security personnel at short notice following a change in venue arrangements. The original provider was unable to fulfil the contract and the event was 48 hours away.

Response

TTS FM sourced and briefed SIA-licensed staff within 48 hours, providing front-of-house and crowd management cover. All personnel were briefed on venue layout, emergency procedures and expected crowd profile.

Event ran without incident. Client retained TTS FM for subsequent events.
Combined Service

Integrated security and site support package

Challenge

A commercial site operator needed both security and basic facilities services but was managing two separate providers. Communication between them was poor, creating gaps in service and accountability.

Response

TTS FM provided an integrated package combining guarding, cleaning and site support under one contract. A single point of contact managed the full scope of requirements.

Simplified operations, clearer accountability and reduced overall cost for the client.
