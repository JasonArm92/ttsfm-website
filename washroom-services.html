How Our Washroom Services Work

Our managed washroom service process is designed to be seamless, discreet, and hassle-free — so you can focus on running your business while we take care of your hygiene requirements.

1. Site Assessment
We begin with a thorough assessment of your washroom facilities, understanding the volume of users, the types of hygiene products required, and any compliance or waste disposal obligations that apply to your site.
2. Service Plan & Schedule
Based on the site assessment, we develop a tailored service plan and schedule — agreeing visit frequencies, product specifications, and service standards that are right for your business.
3. Regular Servicing
Our uniformed service operatives visit your site on the agreed schedule, restocking all hygiene products, servicing and sanitising all units, and removing all waste in full compliance with UK regulations.
4. Quality Review & Reporting
We provide regular quality reviews and service reports, giving you full visibility of the service delivered. Any issues are flagged and resolved promptly to ensure continuous compliance and hygiene standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a managed washroom service include?
A managed washroom service from TTS FM includes the supply, installation, and regular servicing of all washroom hygiene units — including sanitary waste bins, soap dispensers, air fresheners, paper product dispensers, and feminine hygiene units. We also handle all waste collection and disposal in compliance with UK regulations.
How often do you visit for washroom servicing?
Service visit frequencies are tailored to the specific needs of your site and the volume of washroom users. We offer weekly, fortnightly, and monthly service schedules, and can also provide reactive call-out services where required.
Do you supply feminine hygiene units?
Yes. TTS FM supplies, installs, and regularly services feminine hygiene units across all commercial washroom environments. All waste collected from our feminine hygiene units is disposed of in full compliance with UK waste disposal regulations.
Are your washroom services compliant with waste regulations?
Absolutely. All waste collected as part of our managed washroom service — including sanitary waste and clinical waste — is handled and disposed of in full compliance with the Environmental Protection Act 1990 and associated UK waste management regulations. We provide duty of care documentation on request.
Can you service multiple sites across the UK?
Yes. TTS FM has the operational coverage and management infrastructure to deliver consistent managed washroom services across multiple sites anywhere in the UK, under a single service agreement with a dedicated account management team.
