How Our Facilities Management Works

We follow a structured, consultative process to ensure every FM contract is built on a clear understanding of your estate — and delivered with the planning, response and reporting that keep standards high.

1. Discovery & Site Audit
We begin with a thorough audit of your estate — surveying buildings, plant, assets and existing service arrangements to understand your operational priorities, compliance obligations and the issues you want to solve.
2. Integrated FM Plan
From that baseline we design a tailored, integrated FM plan that bundles the right hard and soft services, sets clear service levels and builds a CAFM-driven planned maintenance and compliance schedule around your needs and budget.
3. Mobilisation & Help Desk Setup
We manage a structured mobilisation — onboarding teams, loading assets and PPM tasks into our CAFM system, and setting up your single help desk so every planned and reactive request is logged and routed from day one.
4. Delivery, Reporting & Continuous Improvement
Your account manager oversees delivery against agreed KPIs, with transparent reporting on maintenance, compliance and response performance. Regular reviews drive continuous improvement, cost savings and a service that evolves with your estate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is integrated facilities management?

Integrated facilities management is the delivery of all your building services — both hard and soft FM — through a single, coordinated contract and one accountable provider. Rather than managing separate suppliers for maintenance, compliance, cleaning, security and grounds, you work with one partner, one help desk and one point of contact, reducing cost and supplier complexity.

What is the difference between hard and soft FM?

Hard FM covers the physical building and its systems — building fabric, mechanical and electrical (M&E) services and planned and reactive maintenance — which are often subject to statutory compliance. Soft FM covers the people-focused services that keep a building pleasant and functional, such as cleaning, security, grounds maintenance and washroom services. TTS FM delivers both under one integrated contract.

Can I bundle security and cleaning into one FM contract?

Yes. Our 'One Stop Shop' model is designed to bundle soft services such as security, cleaning, grounds and washroom alongside hard FM like maintenance and M&E — all under a single integrated contract. This gives you one help desk, one point of contact and one transparent view of cost across every service line.

Do you manage statutory compliance and certification?

Yes. We take full ownership of statutory compliance, scheduling and recording all required testing, inspections and certification through our CAFM platform. This keeps your assets compliant and provides a complete, audit-ready trail, supported by our ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 accredited management systems.

Do you provide a single point of contact and reporting?

Absolutely. Every contract is overseen by a dedicated account manager and supported by a single 24/7 help desk that logs and routes all planned and reactive requests. You receive transparent reporting against agreed KPIs covering maintenance, compliance and response performance, with complete visibility across your entire estate.
