How Our Consumables Supply Works

We follow a structured, consultative process to ensure your consumables are matched to how your sites actually operate — then delivered with the stock management and account support that keep you stocked and on budget.

1. Usage Review & Site Survey
We review your current usage, products and dispensers across each site to understand exactly what you need, how much you get through, and where consolidation or savings can be made.
2. Tailored Product Schedule
We agree a bespoke product schedule and pricing, blending trusted brands with cost-effective and eco-friendly options to match your standards, your dispensers and your budget.
3. Managed Stock & Delivery
We monitor usage and operate scheduled deliveries so the right consumables arrive at the right sites before you run short — removing the disruption and premium cost of emergency ordering.
4. Ongoing Account Management
A dedicated account contact reviews usage, consolidates invoicing and refines your schedule over time — keeping spend transparent, predictable and aligned with your changing needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What consumables does TTS FM supply?

We supply the full range of facilities and hygiene consumables, including washroom products, paper and hygiene products, cleaning chemicals, janitorial supplies, and PPE and workplace safety products. Everything can be sourced through a single account and integrated with your cleaning and washroom services.

Can you manage stock levels and replenishment for us?

Yes. We monitor your usage and operate scheduled deliveries so stock arrives before you run out. Managed replenishment removes the burden of ordering from your team, prevents empty dispensers and avoids the premium cost and disruption of emergency orders.

Do you offer eco-friendly and sustainable products?

Absolutely. Alongside trusted brands, we offer a range of eco-friendly and sustainable options, from recycled paper and refillable systems to environmentally responsible chemicals — helping you reduce environmental impact and meet your sustainability goals without compromising on performance.

Can consumables be combined with a cleaning or washroom contract?

Yes. Our consumables supply is designed to integrate seamlessly with our cleaning and washroom services, so products match the systems and dispensers already in place. Combining services under one contract reduces cost, complexity and the overhead of managing multiple suppliers.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes. With regional operating hubs and locally based teams, we deliver consumables across the UK — combining national buying power with reliable, area-based delivery and replenishment for consistent supply wherever your sites are located.
