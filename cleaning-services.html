How Our Cleaning Services Process Works

We follow a structured approach to ensure every cleaning contract delivers consistent, high-quality results. From initial site survey to ongoing account management, we make the process straightforward and transparent for our clients.

1. Site Survey & Assessment
We begin with a thorough site survey to understand your facility, identify specific cleaning requirements, and assess any particular challenges or compliance needs. This allows us to build a service plan that's right for your environment.
2. Tailored Cleaning Plan
Based on the assessment, we design a bespoke cleaning programme — covering frequencies, methods, products, and staffing. We work around your operational hours to minimise disruption while maximising results.
3. Trained Team Deployment
We assign a dedicated cleaning team to your site, briefed and equipped for your specific requirements. All operatives are trained to our quality standards and undergo regular refresher training to maintain excellence.
4. Quality Checks & Reporting
Regular quality inspections and client reviews ensure standards are maintained and any issues are addressed promptly. You'll receive transparent reporting and a single point of contact for all queries and feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cleaning services does TTS FM provide?

TTS FM provides a full range of commercial cleaning services including office cleaning, retail and commercial cleaning, industrial cleaning, carpet and upholstery cleaning, window cleaning, and hard floor care. We also offer specialist deep clean services and one-off cleaning contracts across the UK.

Can you accommodate cleaning outside of business hours?

Yes. We offer flexible scheduling to suit your operational needs, including early morning, evening, and weekend cleaning slots. Our teams work around your business to ensure minimal disruption to your staff and customers.

Are your cleaning products safe and environmentally friendly?

We use cleaning products that comply with COSHH regulations and are safe for use in all commercial environments. We also offer eco-friendly, biodegradable alternatives on request, supporting your sustainability goals without compromising cleaning standards.

How do you ensure consistent quality in your cleaning services?

We have a robust quality management process including regular site inspections, client feedback reviews, and supervisor spot-checks. Each client is assigned a dedicated account manager who monitors performance and ensures any issues are resolved quickly.

Do you provide cleaning services for industrial and warehouse facilities?

Yes. We have specialist teams trained for industrial cleaning environments, including warehouses, manufacturing sites, and logistics facilities. Our industrial cleaning services comply with all relevant health and safety regulations and focus on maintaining safe, clean operational areas.
