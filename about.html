About TTS FM

Professional, operational,
North East founded.

TTS FM is a security and facilities management company based in South Shields. We provide professional guarding, site support and integrated FM services for commercial, industrial and operational sites across the UK.

Who We Are

A security and FM company
that operates directly.

TTS FM was established in the North East to provide security and facilities services to sites that need reliable, professional cover — not agencies, not layers of subcontractors, not a call centre. A company that operates directly and takes accountability seriously.

We are SIA Approved and operate from our South Shields head office, with regional support centres in Edinburgh, Manchester, Birmingham and London. Our work covers commercial property, industrial sites, NHS estates, ports and vacant property across the UK.

Our Approach

Security and FM
under one contract.

Most sites need both security and facilities support. Managing two separate providers creates gaps in accountability and communication. TTS FM delivers both under a single, managed contract.

📋

✓

Why Clients Choose TTS FM

Reliability is not a selling point.
It is the baseline.

We do not pitch on price and cut corners later. We are transparent about what we deliver and consistent in how we deliver it.

Direct communication

Named operations managers. Direct lines. When there is a problem on site, you speak to someone who knows your site — not a call centre operative reading from a script.

Properly briefed personnel

Every officer deployed to a site receives a site-specific brief. They know the access procedures, the escalation protocols and what is expected of them before they arrive.

Structured reporting

Accurate, timely logs and reports as standard. If something happens on site, it is documented correctly. Our clients are never left guessing about what occurred during a shift.

Standards maintained

We do not lower our standards to fill a rota. We would rather cover operationally than deploy someone who is not ready. Our clients notice the difference.
