Who We Are

TTS FM (UK) — Integrated Security & FM Solutions

TTS FM (UK) is a local, regional and nationwide provider of Integrated Risk Based Security Solutions and Facilities Management Services. Leading the industry through most advanced innovative and cost effective solutions, we drive value by applying innovative solutions focused on excellent customer service.

We use our insight and knowledge to create the most appropriate service solution for each client, taking into account the risk profile and culture of the organisation. We create a sustainable service using the latest technologies and methodologies.

WHY CHOOSE TTS FM?

We are one of the UK's leading SIA Approved Integrated Risk Based Security Solutions and Facilities Contractors, all our clients can rest assured that their security and facilities services are being managed by a professionally accredited company.

TTS FM thrives on taking ownership, working hard, leading by example and delivering solutions for our customers. We continually invest in training and development of our staff, remain focused on their welfare, health and safety needs and manage with passion and enthusiasm. Security Services:

  • We are a SIA Approved Contractor and ISO 9001 & 14001 registered company
  • We offer Screening and Vetting to British Standard:7858 covering our Security Officers
  • We offer a comprehensive insurance including £10m public liability and professional indemnity
  • We offer regular Security Officer site audits in line with British Standard:7499 Manned Guarding practices
  • We offer Monthly Management Key Performance Indicator meetings, audits and reviews by our Operation Account Managers
  • We offer a 24-hour Control Station located at Head Office
  • We offer Integrated Security Solutions which support our physical manned guarding teams at their Security assignments
  • We offer a 360 Communications policy covering Security Officers, Supervisors and customers into our Senior Leadership
  • We offer Technology Aided Support platforms reporting all TTS FM operational activities with customer on-line portal access
  • We offer a guaranteed security cover 24 hours a day, 365 days a year
  • We offer real time staff monitoring, proof of patrols and attendance
  • We are CHAS – Health & Safety qualified
  • We are 'train the trainer' qualified providing a raft of Security Industry related training to all of our employees
  • We are an established local, regional and national company with a strong experienced management team
