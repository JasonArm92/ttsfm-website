WHO WE ARE

Integrated Security & Facilities Management Solutions

TTS FM (UK) is a local, regional and nationwide provider of Integrated Risk Based Security Solutions and Facilities Management Services. Leading the industry through the most advanced, innovative and cost-effective solutions, we drive value by applying innovative solutions focused on excellent customer service.

We use our insight and knowledge to create the most appropriate service solution for each client, taking into account the risk profile and culture of the organisation. We create a sustainable service using the latest technologies and methodologies.

We are one of the UK's leading SIA Approved Integrated Risk Based Security Solutions and Facilities Contractors. All our clients can rest assured that their security and facilities services are being managed by a professionally accredited company.

TTS FM thrives on taking ownership, working hard, leading by example and delivering solutions for our customers. We continually invest in the training and development of our staff, remain focused on their welfare, health and safety needs, and manage with passion and enthusiasm. Our experienced teams apply 'best practice' built on over fifty years of operating experience — ensuring our valued partners, our customers, receive the service they deserve and are promised. Every day, we strive for "Excellence in our Service delivery".

Our Journey

A Track Record Built on Experience

From a regional security specialist to a nationwide integrated security and facilities management partner.

Foundations

Built on Manned Guarding Heritage

TTS FM grew from a foundation of professional manned guarding, applying British Standard:7499 practices and building long-term partnerships rooted in trust and accountability.

SIA Approval

SIA Approved Contractor Status

We achieved Security Industry Authority Approved Contractor accreditation — the gold standard for UK security providers — alongside ISO 9001 & 14001 registration and CHAS health & safety qualification.

Integration

Integrated Risk Based Solutions

We expanded into integrated security solutions, supporting our physical manned guarding teams with technology-aided platforms, a 24-hour control station and a customer online portal.

Expansion

Nationwide Regional Coverage

From South Shields to Scotland, the North West, the Midlands and the South, our regional hubs now deliver local, regional and national security and facilities management services.

Today

Excellence in Service Delivery

With over fifty years of combined operating experience, we deliver integrated security and FM across the UK — driven by passion, commitment and dedication to our valued partners.

Leadership Team

Led by Experience, Driven by Values

Our experienced senior leadership team manages with passion and enthusiasm, leading by example and applying best practice across every contract. A 360 communications policy connects our Security Officers, Supervisors and customers directly into our Senior Leadership.

Senior Leadership

An established local, regional and national company with a strong, experienced management team — taking ownership, working hard and delivering solutions for our customers.

Operations Account Management

Our Operation Account Managers run monthly Management Key Performance Indicator meetings, audits and reviews, ensuring standards are maintained and partnerships continue to grow.

24-Hour Control Station

Training & Development
