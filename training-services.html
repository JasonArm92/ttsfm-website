Our Training Approach

Our training programmes are structured to deliver practical, relevant learning that prepares individuals for the demands of their role — not just the demands of the assessment.

1. SIA Licence-Linked Courses
Door supervisor and security guard courses that meet SIA requirements for those seeking or renewing an SIA licence. Includes conflict management, physical intervention, first aid, and legislation modules — delivered by trainers with frontline security experience.
2. Health & Safety Training
NEBOSH, IOSH, and site-specific health and safety training to ensure your teams understand and comply with UK health and safety legislation. Suitable for operatives, supervisors, and managers across all industry sectors.
3. First Aid & Emergency Response
Nationally accredited first aid courses for the workplace, including Level 3 First Aid at Work, Emergency First Aid at Work, and paediatric first aid training. All courses are delivered by qualified first aid instructors and meet HSE requirements.
4. Specialist Technical Training
Bespoke technical training programmes tailored to the specific operational needs of your business, including CCTV operative training, lone worker safety, fire safety awareness, and manual handling — designed and delivered to meet your exact requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What SIA training courses does TTS FM offer?
TTS FM offers SIA licence-linked training courses for door supervisors, security guards, and CCTV operatives. Our courses are designed to meet the SIA's licensing criteria and cover all required units, including conflict management, physical intervention (for door supervisors), first aid, and relevant legislation.
Are your training courses nationally recognised?
Yes. TTS FM delivers nationally recognised qualifications across all of our training programmes, including SIA licence-linked awards, NEBOSH and IOSH health and safety qualifications, and nationally accredited first aid certifications. All qualifications are recognised by the relevant UK awarding bodies and regulatory authorities.
Can you deliver training at our premises?
Yes. TTS FM can deliver training at your own premises, providing a convenient and cost-effective solution for organisations looking to train multiple members of staff. We are also able to deliver training at our own dedicated training facilities. Please contact us to discuss your requirements.
How long do SIA licence courses take to complete?
The duration of SIA licence-linked courses varies depending on the licence type. The Door Supervisor course typically takes around 6 weeks to complete (including both classroom and practical elements), while the Security Guard course is typically shorter. Contact us for exact durations and course schedules.
Do you offer refresher and CPD training?
Yes. TTS FM offers refresher training and CPD (Continuing Professional Development) programmes for individuals and organisations looking to maintain their qualifications, keep their skills current, and demonstrate ongoing professional development. Contact us to discuss your CPD requirements.
