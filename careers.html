Life at TTS FM

Why Work With Us

We are only as good as the people who wear our uniform — so we invest in them. From day-one training to long-term progression, TTS FM is a place where committed people build a genuine career.

Training & Development

We are 'train the trainer' qualified and deliver a full programme of SIA, health & safety and role-specific training. From your first shift onward, you will have the qualifications and support to do the job properly and grow your skills.

Career Progression

We promote from within wherever we can. Many of our supervisors, account managers and trainers started on the front line — if you bring the commitment, we will help you build a long-term career with clear routes to advance.

Supportive Team Culture

Passion, commitment and dedication are more than words to us. Our 360 communications policy connects officers and operatives directly to senior leadership, and our 24-hour control room means you are never working alone.

Competitive Pay & Benefits

We are proud to recognise the value our people bring with fair, competitive rates of pay and a benefits package that reflects the professionalism we expect. Good people deserve to be looked after, and we make sure they are.

Variety of Roles

Across three specialist divisions you could turn your hand to security, concierge, cleaning, grounds maintenance, control-room operations and more. No two days look the same, and there is always a new skill to master.

Nationwide Opportunities

With our head office in South Shields and regional hubs in Scotland, the North West, the Midlands and the South, we offer roles across the UK — so wherever you are based, there is likely an opportunity close to home.

The Process

How to Join Us

Joining TTS FM is straightforward. Here is what to expect from the moment you send us your CV to the day you start your career with us.

1 — Browse & Apply
Send us your CV using the form on this page, or get in touch about a specific role. Tell us a little about your experience and the kind of work you are looking for, and we will match you against current and upcoming opportunities.
2 — Interview & Vetting
If your background fits, we will invite you for an interview to get to know you. As an SIA Approved Contractor we screen and vet to British Standard 7858, so we will guide you through the checks needed to work in security and facilities management.
3 — Onboarding & Training
Once you are offered a role, we get you set up with the right qualifications, uniform and site information. Our 'train the trainer' team delivers the SIA, health & safety and role-specific training you need to start confidently and safely.
4 — Start Your Career
You join the team with the backing of our 24-hour control room, supportive supervisors and a clear route to progress. From your first shift, you are part of a company that invests in its people for the long term.
