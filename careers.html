Typical Roles

Security and site support
positions.

We recruit for a range of operational roles. SIA licence required for all front-line security positions.

SIA-Licensed Security Officer

Static and mobile guarding duties on commercial and industrial sites. Responsible for access control, incident reporting and site security. Front-line, customer-facing role requiring a professional standard of conduct.

SIA Licence Required

Mobile Patrol Officer

Vehicle-based patrol rounds across multiple sites. Responsible for security checks, incident reporting and maintaining patrol logs. Must hold a valid SIA licence and full driving licence.

SIA + Driving Licence

Keyholding & Response Officer

Out-of-hours alarm response across a portfolio of client sites. Must be reliable, available for callout and able to respond professionally to alarm activations at any time.

SIA Licence Required

Site Support Operative

Facilities and site support duties including cleaning, general site tasks and presentation maintenance. No security licence required. Must be reliable and able to work to a consistent standard.

No Licence Required

Gatehouse Operative

Access control and visitor management at managed entry points. Responsible for contractor sign-in, access logs and site security procedures. Requires a professional manner and attention to detail.

SIA Licence Required

Standards

What we expect
from our staff.

We do not lower our standards to fill shifts. We would rather run short and cover operationally than put someone on site who is not ready. The expectations below are non-negotiable.

✓ SIA licence required for all front-line security roles
✓ Punctuality and reliability are non-negotiable — missing shifts without notice is not acceptable
✓ Professional presentation and conduct on site at all times
✓ Clear and accurate reporting — logs must be completed correctly and on time
✓ Commitment to following site-specific instructions and client procedures
✓ Communication — if there is a problem, you contact your manager. No surprises.

If you meet these standards — get in touch.

We are looking for professional, reliable individuals who want consistent work and clear expectations. Contact TTS FM to discuss availability and current requirements.
