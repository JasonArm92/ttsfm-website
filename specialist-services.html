Technical Expertise for Specialist & Safety-Critical Tasks

From rope access and height safety to confined space entry and high-pressure jetting, TTS FM deploys specialist teams with the training, accreditation, and equipment required to work safely in some of the most demanding environments in the UK.

Every specialist task we undertake is preceded by thorough risk assessments and method statements, ensuring that all work is planned, controlled, and executed in full compliance with UK health and safety legislation. Our clients trust us to get the job done right — on time, safely, and without compromise.

  • IRATA certified rope access operatives
  • Confined space trained teams with rescue capability
  • Full risk assessments and method statements provided
  • Work at height specialist equipment and PPE
  • Compliance with UK health and safety legislation

Where We Deliver Specialist Services

TTS FM's specialist teams operate across a wide range of sectors and environments, bringing technical expertise and safety-critical capability to every project we take on.

Commercial Buildings

Height access for facades, roofs, and glazing on commercial buildings — delivering maintenance and inspection work that conventional access methods cannot safely reach.

Industrial Facilities

Confined space entry and specialist access for plant maintenance in industrial environments, with full rescue capability and compliance documentation at every stage.

Utilities & Infrastructure

Drain clearing, pressure jetting, and pipe maintenance for utilities and infrastructure clients — tackling blockages, scale, and debris with specialist high-pressure equipment.

Construction Sites

Rope access and height safety support during active construction phases, providing access solutions and fall protection for projects where conventional scaffolding is not viable.

Retail & Leisure

Specialist cleaning and maintenance at height or in restricted areas across retail and leisure venues — minimising disruption while delivering the access solutions your property needs.

Education & Healthcare

Safety-critical maintenance in environments where conventional access is not possible, carried out with minimal disruption and full compliance with the requirements of education and healthcare settings.
