How Our Waste Service Works

We follow a structured process to ensure every waste contract is compliant, efficient and aligned with your sustainability goals — built on a clear understanding of your waste streams and supported by transparent reporting.

1. Waste Audit & Site Survey
We carry out a detailed audit of your premises to understand the volumes, types and frequency of waste you produce. This survey identifies opportunities to segregate materials, increase recycling and reduce unnecessary cost.
2. Tailored Waste & Recycling Plan
We design a bespoke plan covering bin provision, collection schedules and the waste streams to be separated. Every plan is built to maximise landfill diversion while keeping your duty-of-care obligations fully covered.
3. Scheduled Collection & Compliance
Licensed carriers collect your waste on an agreed schedule and transport it to approved treatment facilities. Waste transfer notes and consignment documentation are completed for every collection, keeping your records audit-ready.
4. Reporting & Continuous Improvement
We provide regular reporting on volumes, recycling rates and landfill diversion, and review performance with you over time. Together we identify further ways to reduce waste at source and drive ongoing improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

What waste management services do you provide?

We provide a full range of commercial waste services including general waste collection, mixed and dry recycling, confidential shredding, hazardous and clinical waste, WEEE recycling, and skip and bulk collection. Services can be delivered individually or as part of an integrated facilities management contract.

How do you ensure duty-of-care compliance?

We manage your legal duty of care from end to end. All waste is collected by licensed carriers and taken to approved treatment facilities, and we complete waste transfer notes and consignment documentation for every collection. This gives you a clear, audit-ready record demonstrating that your waste has been handled and disposed of lawfully.

Can you help us divert waste from landfill?

Yes. Our service is built around recycling and recovery. We begin with a waste audit to identify the materials you can segregate, then design a plan to maximise recycling and divert as much waste as possible from landfill. Regular reporting on recycling rates and diversion helps you track progress against your sustainability goals.

Do you handle hazardous and confidential waste?

We do. We handle hazardous, clinical and WEEE waste in line with the relevant regulations, providing consignment notes and treatment at approved facilities. For confidential material, we offer secure document shredding and destruction, with certificates of destruction provided so your records remain complete and compliant.

Can waste management be included in a wider FM contract?

Absolutely. Our 'One Stop Shop' model lets you combine waste management with cleaning, maintenance, security and wider FM under a single contract and one point of contact — reducing cost, complexity and the overhead of managing multiple suppliers while keeping standards consistent.
