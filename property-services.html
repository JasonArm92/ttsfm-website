Frequently Asked Questions — FAQ

What are property services?

Property services involve the professional maintenance, repair, and care of commercial and residential buildings. This includes everything from routine inspections and minor repairs to complex electrical or plumbing work, ensuring your property stays in peak condition.

What is the difference between property services and facilities management?

While facilities management often covers the broader "people and process" side (like cleaning and security), property services focus specifically on the physical building. This includes building fabric repairs, mechanical and electrical maintenance, and general property upkeep.

Why are professional property services important for UK businesses?

Using a professional service like TTS FM ensures your building remains fully compliant with UK building regulations and safety standards. Regular maintenance prevents small issues from turning into expensive structural problems, protecting your long-term investment.

Does TTS FM provide reactive property repairs?

Yes. We offer both planned preventative maintenance and reactive repair services. Our local teams in London, Manchester, Birmingham, and Newcastle are equipped to respond quickly to urgent issues, ensuring your property remains safe and operational.

How are property service costs calculated?

Costs are based on the specific needs of your site, including the age of the building, the frequency of visits required, and the scope of work (such as electrical, plumbing, or general repairs). We provide transparent, scalable pricing to suit your budget.
