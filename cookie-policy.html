Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and how you can control them

This site uses cookies — small text files that are placed on your machine to help the site provide a better user experience. In general, cookies are used to retain user preferences, store information for things like shopping carts, and provide anonymised tracking data to third party applications like Google Analytics.

As a rule, cookies will make your browsing experience better. However, you may prefer to disable cookies on this site and on others.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your preferences and improve your experience over time. Cookies do not give us access to your computer or any information about you beyond what you choose to share with us.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to understand how visitors use our website, to remember your preferences, and to improve the overall experience. Some cookies are essential for the site to function correctly, while others help us analyse traffic and tailor content to your interests.

We may use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics which place their own cookies to track visitor usage. These cookies help us understand which pages are most popular and how visitors navigate our site. No personally identifiable information is collected through these cookies.

How to Disable Cookies

The most effective way to disable cookies is through your browser settings. We suggest consulting the Help section of your browser or visiting the About Cookies website, which offers guidance for all modern browsers.

Please be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites that you visit.
